Project Overview

The OpenSpace International Operator Impact Assessment Platform provides space operators with comprehensive tools to analyze conjunction events, calculate economic impacts, and make informed decisions about collision avoidance maneuvers.

This MVP demonstrates the core concepts of economic-based debris prioritization, helping operators:

  • Assess collision risks in real-time
  • Calculate the true economic cost of debris threats
  • Make data-driven decisions on collision avoidance
  • Identify high-priority debris for removal missions
  • Generate business cases for active debris removal
